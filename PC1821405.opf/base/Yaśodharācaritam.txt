yaśodharācaritamśrī siddhārthaḥ kapilanagare bodhisattvaḥ saputrāṃdevīṃ tyaktvā sakalavibhavaṃ
cakravartiśriyaṃ ca |māsy āṣāḍhe viratamanasā pūrṇimāyāṃ niśītheniścakrāma śramaṇapadabhṛn
mokṣam anveṣṭukāmaḥ

atha prabhāte śayanāt samutthitāsutena sārdhaṃ mahiṣī yaśodharā |didṛkṣayā svāmivarasya satvaraṃgatāvidūre
śayanīyamandiram

yathā pure vañcayituṃ sa me patīrahogataḥ kutra cid eva gūḍhavān |vicintya saudhe 'ntarabāhiraṃ
bhṛśaṃparīkṣyamāṇāpi dadarśa naiva tam

dadhāva tasmāt sacivasya mandiraṃgavākṣam udghāṭya drutaṃ drutaṃ śucā |kapāṭam āhatya dṛḍhaṃ
muhur muhurvyarīravac channa bhavan bhavann iti

apekṣya kiṃ cit kvaṇam antarād gṛhevyatiṣṭhatālpaṃ pratihārasannidhau |vicārabhaṅgāc chvasatī
ca pāṇināpratāḍayām āsa kapolayugmakam

tato gatā sā nṛpamandurālayaṃturaṅgamaṃ nātra dadarśa kanthakam |kuto yayau kiṃ rahasā sa vallabhovitarkayantīti
babhūva mūrchitā

vicintya yat kiṃ cid itas tataḥ śiraścacāla jaghrau ca mukhe sutasya sā |bhujātapatreṇa vilocanadvayānnyavārayat
sā sravadaśruśīkaram

aśaknuvantī nitarāṃ samudgamāṃmanovyathāṃ soḍhum udagramanyunā |patipradattaṃ maṇikaṇṭhabhūṣaṇaṃpravālamālām
api kuṇḍaladvayam

lalāṭikāṃ hemamayāṅgulīyakaṃhiraṇyasūtreṇa nibaddhamekhalām |vimucya śīrṣābharaṇaṃ ca kaṅkaṇesasarja
dhig dhig vadatī sasambhramam

atītaḍat pādatalena bhūtalaṃjajṛmbha ūrdhvaṃ vicakarṣa mūrdhajam |jajalpa vākyaṃ vigatārtham
ākulaṃrurāva bhīteva ruroda gadgadam

niveśya bālaṃ śayane punaḥ punaḥsamutkṣipantī yugapat karadvayam |vikampya śīrṣaṃ ca yathā vicetanājughoṣa
śaśvāsa papāta vivyathe

nipīḍyamānāpi bubhukṣayāniśaṃna bhojanaṃ bhoktum iyeṣa kiṃ cana |tṛṣāturā nodakabindum apy asaupapau
ca kāye 'py babhūva vepathuḥ

nihanyate dhīsmṛtiśaktidhīratāśarīravarṇaṃ balam indriyeṣv api |vinaśyate jīvam anukrameṇa cadurantateyaṃ
priyaviprayogatā

niṣadya bhūmyāṃ vinatārdhavigrahāśiraś ca pāṇidvayam aṅkamastake |niveśya kiṃ cit pariṇamya
kaṃdharaṃmamajja cintājaladhāv apāśraye

yaśodharāyā avamānanakriyāasahya siddhārthakumāra ekakaḥ |nivāsam utsṛjya gato nu kānanamiti
pravādo 'pi bhaved aho mama

yad asti kāryaṃ triṣu mandireṣu taccakāra kārya- kṣamasevikā yathā |gatau sthitau vā śayane
ca vipriyaṃkadā canāhaṃ na cakāra kiṃ cana

vicintayantī sahasāgatāpadaṃpragamya devāyatanaṃ priyaṃ prati |sugandhipuspair api dhūmadīpakairapūpujad
devagaṇaṃ sagauravam

bhujadvayaṃ mūrdhni niveśya śokinīvilokya devapratimānanaśriyam |praṇamya bhaktyā pariśuddhacetasāyayāca
devān iti viprayogataḥ

trilokasargasthitināśahetavaḥcaturmukhaśrīpaticandraśekharāḥ |mahānubhāvā jagadīśvarāḥ surāḥsuduḥkhitāyai
śaraṇaṃ bhavantu me

vidhāya yācñām iti devahastayorupāyanīkṛtya babandha kārṣikau |upāharan miṣṭanavāmbupāyasaṃsitāṃśukaṃ
svarṇamayīṃ ca putrikām

pramodam āpādayituṃ sureśvarāṃścakāra ghaṇṭāninadaṃ ca bhūsurān |ajījapad devagirā ca saṃstavānnanāma
bhūmāv asakṛt nipatya sā

pure vane saṃvasathe 'pi vallabhovased dhi kasminn iti divyācakṣuṣā |vilokya gehaṃ vinivartayantu
taṃdivaukasaḥ prārthayataiva sā punaḥ

nirīkṣya jihmānimiṣeṇa cakṣuṣānihatya gāḍhaṃ caraṇaṃ bhuvas tale |muhur muhur daṃśitakomalādharāprakarṣam
utkṣipya vikampya tarjanīm

are-tvayāryasya gṛhābhiniṣkramaḥśruto na dṛṣṭaḥ vada kiṃ nirākulam |iti pratīhārabhaṭaṃ ruṣoccakairaveṇa
papraccha sabāṣpalocanā

bhavān sa ārye nṛpavaṃśaśekharomayā na dṛṣṭaḥ pratihāram āgataḥ |varāṅganābhiḥ saha kelikānanegate
niśīthe 'ramateti me śrutam

avītarāgo nanu rājanandanovavāñcha tasmān nanu nūtanapriyām |mithaḥ sa kāṃ cit pariṇīya nāṭikāṃbahir
gato 'ddheti vitarkayāmy aham

purā hi mandhātv-abhidho mahāyaśāḥsa sārvabhaumo 'pi surāṅganāgaṇe |kṛtānurāgo parivarjya mānuṣīṃśriyaṃ
prapede nu divaukasāṃ puram

mano-'bhirāmaṃ surasundarīgaṇaṃvihāya vindhyā-vanatāpasāśrame |agastipatnīṃ parirabhya tāpasīṃlalāsa
rantuṃ niśi pākaśāsanaḥ

pratīta-laṅkāvijite 'grabhūpatirdaśānano daṇḍakakānanaṃ gataḥ |vigṛhya sītāṃ janakātmajām apinijapriyāvad
vyadadhāt tadātmasāt

maheśvaraś cāpi purā tapaś carannumāmahiṣyā saha kalikānane |apāsya sātatyasamādhibhāvanaṃvyarīramac
cājanayat sutau nanu

śucākulāṃ sāntvayituṃ vadhūttamāmiti pratīhāra uraḥ-sthale 'ñjalim |vidhāya valgudhvaninānukampayānatena
mūrdhnā vacasā vyajijñapat

are-tvam etarhi bhavantam uttamaṃgṛhe bahir vopavane 'pi kutra cit |aharmukhe kiṃ niśi vā dadarśithavyapṛcchad
itthaṃ nijasevikāṃ ruṣā

nidhāya vāmetarajānumaṇḍalaṃkṣitau bhujau cāpi lalāṭamastake |natārdhakāyaṃ nijagāda sevikāśrutaṃ
ca dṛṣṭaṃ na na vedmi kiṃ cana

ataḥ-paraṃ harmyam upeyuṣī satīpramārjya nisyandajalaṃ vilocane |are-kim āryo 'tra samāgato
na vāvyapṛcchad antaḥpurapālapaṇḍakam

praśasyarūpeṇa girā ca valgunāśriyā 'pi dhṛtyā tava kakṣam āgatāḥ |mahottame sarvajanena mānitābhavanti
tasya tritayāḥ priyāṅganāḥ

mano-'bhirāmā bhavatī ca bhāratītathaiva lakṣmīr iti tāḥ priyāṅganāḥ |sadaiva tābhī ramate kumārakaḥkadā
cid anyatra mano 'sya no rajet

jugupsite satpuruṣair manasvibhiḥsadoṣakāme vimukho nu te patiḥ |tato 'sya śuddhāntagatiḥ kadā
canaśrutā na dṛṣṭā na divā vā niśi

tapodhanānāṃ samatām upeyivānratiṃ na kuryāt sa bhavatraye kva cit |amedhyagehena same 'varodhanekathaṃ
vidhatte ratim eṣa te patiḥ

asādhyarogāyatane sukhetarebubhukṣayā nityanipīḍitodare |atarpaṇīye vividhānnapānakaiḥspṛhāṃ
na dehe prakaroti paṇḍitaḥ

vikīrṇagharmodakavipruṣi tvacivicitravarṇaṃ śamalena saṃskṛte |śarīra ālokya jano vinaśyatepataṅgavad
dīpaśikhojjvalaprabhām

tato nṛpāntaḥ-purapālakoditekṛtāvadhānā vacane yaśodharā |divaukasāṃ nandanakānanopamaṃjagāma
rājopavanaṃ calātmanā

athābhirāmopavanasya rakṣakaḥpuraḥ pragamyorasi baddhapāṇinā |kimartham ārye 'tra samāgatis
tavabhavāmi jijñāsur iti nyavedayat

sakhe mamāryo rahasāruṇodayesamāgato vātra na kiṃ samāgataḥ |kim atra dṛṣṭaḥ sa na vā śrutas
tvayāvicintya samyag vada māṃ yathātatham

gataspṛhaḥ pañcasu kāmavastuṣusa āryaputro vijitendriyo bhavet |tato 'bhirāmopavanaṃ samāgamona
dṛṣṭa ārye na hi vā śruto mayā

vidhitsamāno 'bhyudayaṃ jagattrayedivaukasānām adhigamya yācanam |divo 'vatīrṇo nanu puṇyatejasāmanuṣyajanmany
ajaniṣṭa sa prabhuḥ

mahāmahe saty asitarṣir īyivāndivaṃ kim ity asya vimṛśya kāraṇam |svamitra-śuddhodanabhūpateḥ
suto'janīti harṣāt tam avekṣituṃ yayau

tato 'sitarṣir nṛpasaudham āgataḥkumārakaṃ vīkṣya sapuṇyasampadam |atīndriyajñānamayena cakṣuṣāparīkṣamāṇo
'sya śarīralakṣaṇam

jagāda vākyaṃ śiśur eṣa puṇyavānbhaved dhi buddho hata-mārabandhanaḥ |pratārayet sarvanarāmarāsurānapārasaṃsāramahārṇavād
iti

dvijeṣu pañcasv atha dehalakṣaṇaṃparīkṣya kauṇḍaṇyabudho 'bravīd iti |vinaśya tṛṣṇādimalāny
ayaṃ śiśuranuttaraṃ buddhapadaṃ gamiṣyati

tadā prasūtakṣaṇa eva lumbinī-vane sa saptāmbujamūrdhny abhivrajan |abhītanādaṃ nyanadaj jagattrayebhaveyam
agras tv iti siṃhapotavat

bhramaddvirephaśrutiramyanisvanaḥsugandhinaḥ puṣpaparāgarāśinā |taraṃgiṇīśīkaramiśramārutovavau
samantād vipine śanaiḥ tadā

mahātmanas tasya parārdhyajanmanivasuṃdharāyā nidhayaḥ samudgatāḥ |dharāṅganā tarhy atimātramoditāsavismayāṃ
siddhim adarśayad bhuvi

tataḥ paraṃ lumbinikānanād gṛhaṃvicitrajāmbūnadabimbasaṃnibham |kumārakaṃ tau pitarau savatsalausabandhuvargaṃ
parivārya ninyatuḥ

gurupradiṣṭaṃ vidhivad gurupriyaḥsamastavidyāsamayaṃ ca darśanam |bhave bhave saṃcitapuṇyatejasāsamadhyagīṣṭāciram
eṣa buddhimā

śrutena dhṛtyā navayauvanaśriyamupeyuṣo 'syārya-sutasya janmanaḥ |saha tvayā ṣoḍaśame hi hāyanevivāhadīkṣāṃ
vidhivac cakāra nu
